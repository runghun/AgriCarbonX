What It Is

Agri-carbon credits represent a verified reduction or removal of greenhouse gases (like $\text{CO}_2$) from the atmosphere achieved through *sustainable farming practices*.

How Farmers Earn

By adopting methods like *no-till farming, cover cropping, and optimized fertilizer use, farmers increase soil carbon sequestration. These generate **tradable credits*.

The Benefits

Farmers receive *additional income* from selling credits to companies offsetting their emissions. It also leads to healthier soil and better crop yields.
