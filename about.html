About

Carbon Credit Marketplace for Farmers

Our Mission

We provide a platform that connects farmers with businesses and organizations interested in purchasing carbon credits. Our goal is to support sustainable farming practices and provide farmers with a new source of income.

How It Works

1

Register

Farmers create an account by providing information about their farm and farming practices.

2

Earn Credits

Farmers implement sustainable practices such as tree planting, soil conservation, or organic farming.

3

Sell Credits

Farmers sell their carbon credits to businesses and organizations looking to offset their emissions.
